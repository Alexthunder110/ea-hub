Privacy Policy · Last updated: April 9, 2026

1. Overview

MarketPulse is developed by Alexander Waurig. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

2. Data We Collect

  • Push Notification Token — only if you grant permission, used to send market alerts via OneSignal
  • Local Timezone — used locally on your device to display correct market hours, never transmitted

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No account registration required
  • No personal data (name, email, address)
  • No location data
  • No financial data or trading activity
  • No advertising identifiers

4. Third Party Services

We use OneSignal for push notifications. OneSignal may collect device identifiers to deliver notifications. See OneSignal's privacy policy at: onesignal.com/privacy-policy

5. Data Storage

All data (timezone, settings) is stored locally on your device only. We operate no servers and store no user data externally.

6. Children

MarketPulse is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
